Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) From Employers

Is there any minimum wage requirement that I need to pay my employees?

Yes, each state has its own minimum wage requirement that you would need to pay your employee with.

To know more about your state regulations and to avoid being penalized for law violations, check the Minimum Wage Requirements imposed by the Department of Labor.

How do I pay my employee their remaining wages if I have already paid them below their minimum wage and how would the error be corrected?

To pay your employee their remaining wages due to them being paid incorrectly and below their state's minimum wage requirements, you can sign in to your account and follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to the sidebar and click the Payroll tab.

  2. Under the Type of Payroll to Be Used heading, select Correction Payroll.

  3. Under the Pay Periods that Need Correction headline, select the specific pay periods from the Pay Periods dropdown list to add them.

  4. Under the Employees Affected headline, select the specific employees by selecting them from the Employees dropdown list to add them.

  5. On the next page, enter the following things:

    • New pay period
    • New pay date
    • New payment amount
    • New payment method
  6. On the next page, review the payroll to be submitted. If everything looks good, submit the payroll.

If a form or report has already been submitted to the appropriate tax agencies, we will file a correction within 7 to 14 business days. You will receive a status update on your account as well as an email notification.
